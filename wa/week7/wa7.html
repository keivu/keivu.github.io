Privacy Statement

This privacy statement explains our collection of information, use, and sharing practices of Boulder County Arts Alliance. Please review this privacy policy carefully before submitting any information through our services.

INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE DIRECTLY TO US We will collect any information you provide us. This information can be collected in ways that include making donations, contacting us or giving us feedback, or subscribing to our newsletter. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or geographic location.

COOKIES We collect your use of our services through the use of internet server logs like cookies. Cookies store your preferences and settings, enhance your experience by delivering content specific to your interests. If you receive an email from us (such as the newsletter, we may use certain analytical tools to capture data, such as whether you open our message, click any links or banners in the email, or interact at all with what we send. This data allows us to analyze the effectiveness of our communications and marketing campaigns.
